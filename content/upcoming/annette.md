#### About [Annette](https://www.weizenbaum-institut.de/portrait/p/annette-zimmermann/)
Annette Zimmermann is an Assistant Professor of Philosophy at the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a Guest Researcher at the Weizenbaum Institut. Her research focuses on political philosophy and the moral philosophy of AI. She holds a DPhil from the University of Oxford and is co-founder of the Philosophy of AI & Data Labs. Her awards include the Public Philosophy Prize from the American Philosophical Association (2021) and a Technology and Human Rights Fellowship at Harvard University (2020-23).
