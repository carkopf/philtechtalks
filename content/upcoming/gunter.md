#### About [Gunter](https://www.tue.nl/en/research/researchers/gunter-bombaerts)
Gunter Bombaerts is an Associate Professor at Eindhoven University of Technology in the Department of Philosophy and Ethics. His research interests include ethics of technology, engineering ethics education, and the attention economy. He is affiliated with the Eindhoven Center for the Philosophy of AI (EPHIL.AI).
